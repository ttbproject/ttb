The education camp

Overview of what you can expect

Our camp is established to help you understand and embrace the values of the Chinese state and society. This includes promoting patriotism, loyalty to the Communist Party, and obedience to the laws and regulations of the state.

Political education

You will be taught about the history of the Chinese Communist Party, its role in the development of China, and the importance of supporting and upholding the Party's ideals.

Ideological transformation

You will be encouraged to abandon your previous beliefs and ideologies, and adopt those of the Chinese state. This includes renouncing any ties to terrorism or extremism, and embracing the values of the Chinese nation.

Vocational training

You will be taught various skills and trades that will help you contribute to the Chinese economy and society. These may include manufacturing, agriculture, or other industries.

Physical labor

You will be required to participate in various forms of physical labor, such as manual labor, cleaning, or farming, as part of your training and rehabilitation.

Behavior modification

You will be taught to conform to the rules and regulations of the camp, and to demonstrate obedience and respect to the authorities. Any disobedience or noncompliance will be punished.

We understand that this may be a difficult transition for you,
but we believe that with dedication and hard work, you can become productive and loyal members of the Chinese nation.
